## Notes

- **Customer Supplied** — returnable packaging sent by the customer; no PO generated at receiving.
- Items listed under both sources appeared under PO'd receipts and customer-supplied returns
  at different points in the history.
- The **V-EMB series** are Volvo-specific returnable packaging containers tracked separately
  from general dunnage.
- **Lennox Jackies** are returnable wire containers from the Lennox customer account, recorded
  consistently under that name throughout the history.
- **Genfoam / Gen Walls** and **MPB-prefixed** items are molded foam packaging inserts received
  as purchased parts; the numbered suffixes (1–4) denote different densities or sizes within the
  same foam product family.
