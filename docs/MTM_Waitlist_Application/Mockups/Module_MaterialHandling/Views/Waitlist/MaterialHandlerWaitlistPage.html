Material Handler Workflow
  • Zone Focus: Your lead assigns you to a zone (A/B/C). Red tasks in your zone auto-prioritize.
  • Complete Tasks: Click "Complete" on individual tasks or select multiple and use bulk complete.
  • Red = Urgent: Tasks that exceed time standards turn red automatically.
  • Other Zones: You can see tasks in other zones for awareness but can't complete them.
  • Auto-Assign (Optional): If enabled by leads, red tasks in your zone may auto-assign to you.
Zone Assignment: Contact your Material Handler Lead to change your zone assignment. Zone changes require lead approval.
